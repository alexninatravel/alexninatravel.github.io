Portugal: Day 10 - Lagos

Written by Nina Quach

More beach! After trying a couple, we finally found one that was relatively shielded from the waves. This time I joined for snorkeling and between the waves and the cold, I thought I was going to die. We made it to this rock [photo] and back, and I quickly jumped out of my wetsuit and laid on the beach for the rest of the day instead, while Alex went back. Not much more to say, except that I downloaded a game called Atomas that day and played the whole afternoon like an addict.

At dinner time we went back to the same restaurant and once more, got excellent food and service. And once more, we were too busy enjoying to take pictures (sorry!).
